Frequently Asked Questions

1. How can you print a document from your laptop at HZ?

First go to the HZ print webpage, log in to your account and upload all necessary files to be printed, Go to the printer, scan your card on it, choose the print option and hit print.

2. How can you scan a document a send it to your laptop at HZ

Full instructions here

3. How can I buy something on the HZ web shop?

Answer: Log on to the webshop, find and select what you are looking to buy, add it to your cart. When you are done with the selection, proceed to checkout cart and select preferred payment option and pay

4. Where can I find the latest instructions on what to do when you are sick / show symptoms of COVID?

Answer: Visit this website

5. How can you book a project space in one of the wings?

ANSWER: Log in to the HZ self service portal, select "make a reservation" and follow the instructions.

6. What are the instructions if you want to park your car at the HZ parking lot?

You need to reveal you HZ pass at the barriers so you can get through and into the parking lot. Due to the expansion of the building, the parking spaces are limited and reserved only for those who truly need it.
