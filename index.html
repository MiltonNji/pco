Why HZ University?

HZ University offers students one of the most practical educational programs you could get out there. at HZ University not only are you getting premium education, you are also getting a well recognised certificate because HZ ranks as one if the best universities in the Netherlands and in Europe

HZ also has so many partnerships with universities and companies locally and worldwide so your internships and minors can be done locally and internationally, all this at one of the most cost effective programs in the world. At HZ, you get your money's worth of education and experience and fun all in one package called the HBO-ICT program, and that is what i believe is the STUDENT DREAM

Why the HBO-ICT program

The HBO-ICT program is as practical as practical can get. ICT in general is the cream on the pie for computer enthusiasts because it requires little to no previous knowledge in computer science and most importantly math and physics. The ICT program just requires you to love learning and doing with an open mind. The program structure gives students alot of room to be flexible and share their thoughts and ideas and also develop their personality

The past 2 weeks and also my background in ICT(which i started doing in high school and for the last 3 years University), has made me see and love the challenge that comes with creating something visual that is non tangible but can change people's lives. ICT and computer engineers are the closest to magicians in the real world. I love being a magician

Reasons ICT suits me

  • I love learning while doing.
  • Being able to create new things such as software, apps, websites soothe me. The joy I get from code running succesfully is second to none.
  • The courses taught and the career path choices offered match with my dreams and aspirations
  • With this program, i am going to have a full CV before i graduate, maybe even a job because of the practicality of the program.
